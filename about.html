About our team

The Badminton CCA was established to promote fitness, teamwork, and sportsmanship. We train regularly to compete at zonal and national levels, while supporting each member’s personal growth.

Meet Coach James

With over 15 years of coaching experience, Coach James leads the school's badminton team with passion. He believes strongly in building strong fundamentals, discipline and sportsmanship while ensuring that training remains fun and engaging for students. Our students have not only improve their performance but also grown in confidence and teamwork

Whether you're a beginner or a competitive player, Coach James is committed to helping every student unlock their full potential!
